## Key Terms

**Asset criticality**: A rating of how serious it would be if an asset failed.

**Board assurance**: Clear evidence and reporting that helps senior leaders understand compliance and risk.

**Competent contractor**: A contractor with suitable skills, training, experience, insurance and evidence for the work.

**Compliance Bible**: The companion repo containing legal pages, frameworks, templates, audit checklists and product schema.

**FM Control Hub**: Product concept for managing assets, tasks, evidence, contractors, permits, actions and dashboards.

**Lifecycle planning**: Planning repair, refurbishment or replacement before failure becomes an emergency.

**Operational scenario**: A realistic FM problem used to practise judgement and response.

**Permit to work**: A formal control for higher-risk work such as hot works, isolations, roof access or intrusive works.

**RAG**: Retrieval-augmented generation, where AI learning answers are grounded in source documents and tags.

**RAMS**: Risk assessments and method statements.

**Responsible Person**: A named role with responsibility for a compliance area, such as fire safety or water hygiene.

**STAR**: Situation, Task, Action, Result; a structure for interview answers.

**Visitor attraction FM**: Facilities Management in a live public environment with high footfall, events, public safety and operational reputation risk.
