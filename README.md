## Mission

This repo is a structured Facilities Management course designed to teach practical Level 4-style knowledge through lessons, scenarios, workbook tasks, mock exams, marking guides and final assessment.

It is not an official IWFM qualification. It is a production-grade learning framework designed to help a learner think, write and speak like an operational Facilities Manager.

## Production Rules

- No placeholder modules.
- No empty folders committed just to look complete.
- Every lesson must be readable on a phone.
- Every module must include learning notes, practical application, scenario questions, model answers and marking criteria.
- Every completed task must update `TODO.md` and `CHANGELOG.md`.
- Every commit must be pushed once the task is complete.

## Course Outcomes

By the end, the learner should be able to explain and apply:

- operational FM principles
- risk-based decision making
- health and safety management
- fire safety responsibilities
- water hygiene and Legionella control
- building systems and asset management
- contractor management
- budgeting and lifecycle planning
- leadership and stakeholder communication
- strategic FM planning
- estate management
- visitor attraction and Longleat-style operational scenarios

## Mobile Learning Standard

Lessons should be:

- short enough to read on a phone
- broken into clear sections
- practical rather than academic
- scenario-heavy
- written in plain UK English
- hard enough to prepare for real FM interviews

## Product Integration Vision
