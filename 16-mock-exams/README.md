## Purpose

Two mock exams that test understanding across the whole course under timed, closed-book conditions. They are summative: sit them after working through the modules and workbook.

## How to Sit a Mock

- Allow **90 minutes**, closed book.
- Answer all sections in your own words.
- Section A is short knowledge; Section B is applied; Section C is a scenario that carries the most marks.
- Mark yourself afterwards with the [marking guide](../17-marking-guides/marking-guide.md).
- Record your band and weak tags, then revise those modules.

## Recommended Order

1. Modules 1-12 read.
2. Workbook completed.

4. Mock Exam 2, mark, revise weak tags.
