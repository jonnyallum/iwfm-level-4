> Scenarios 31-40 of the [FM operational scenario bank](../fm-operational-scenario-bank.md). For each, work the [scenario response structure](../fm-operational-scenario-bank.md#scenario-response-structure): make safe, who is affected, legal duty, competent people, evidence, owners, escalation, prevention.

### 31. Waste Carrier Licence Missing

Prompt: A new waste contractor cannot provide carrier registration.

Expected response: do not release waste, verify registration, use approved carrier and record duty of care check.

### 32. Waste Transfer Note Missing

Prompt: Waste collections occurred but transfer notes are missing.

Expected response: request copies, check contractor process, record gap, prevent recurrence and escalate if repeated.

### 33. Pest Activity in Food Area

Prompt: Pest activity is reported near a visitor cafe.

Expected response: inform pest contractor, protect food area, inspect hygiene/building defects, log actions and verify close-out.

### 34. Visitor Path Trip Hazard

Prompt: Uneven paving is found on a high-footfall route.

Expected response: barrier or divert, photograph, risk-rate, repair, inspect similar areas and track completion.

### 35. Tree Limb Over Public Route

Prompt: A cracked tree limb is visible above a visitor path.

Expected response: close/divert route, call arborist, record photos, complete works and review tree inspection regime.

### 36. Animal Enclosure Gate Defect

Prompt: A primary enclosure gate latch is unreliable.

Expected response: notify animal team, restrict use, temporary control, competent repair, senior escalation and evidence.

### 37. Estate Road Flooding

Prompt: Heavy rain floods a service road used by visitors and staff.

Expected response: assess route safety, close/divert, check drainage, communicate, record and plan permanent improvement.

### 38. Public Toilet Outage

Prompt: A main toilet block fails during peak attendance.

Expected response: deploy alternative facilities, communicate, call contractor, manage hygiene, review resilience.

### 39. Power Outage

Prompt: A site-wide power outage occurs with 5,000 visitors onsite.

Expected response: incident command, life-safety systems, generators, visitor movement, animal-critical systems, board updates.

### 40. Generator Fails Test

Prompt: The emergency generator fails its monthly test.

Expected response: assess critical loads, call contractor, temporary contingency, escalate risk and track repair.
