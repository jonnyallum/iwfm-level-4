## Purpose

Use this repo to prepare for Longleat-style FM interviews by practising practical answers, boardroom judgement and visitor attraction scenarios.

## Seven-Day Prep Route

Day 1: Read [FM Principles](01-fm-principles/module-01-fm-principles.md) and [Risk Management](02-risk-management/module-02-risk-management.md).

Day 2: Read [Health & Safety Management](03-health-safety/module-03-health-safety-management.md) and [Fire Safety](04-fire-safety/module-04-fire-safety.md).

Day 3: Read [Water Hygiene](05-water-hygiene/module-05-water-hygiene.md) and [Building Systems](06-building-systems/module-06-building-systems.md).

Day 4: Read [Asset Management](07-asset-management/module-07-asset-management.md) and [Contractor Management](08-contractor-management/module-08-contractor-management.md).

Day 5: Work through [Longleat Boardroom Simulation](13-longleat-simulation/longleat-boardroom-simulation.md).

Day 6: Practise 40 questions from the [Longleat interview question bank](19-interview-prep/longleat-interview-question-bank.md).

Day 7: Practise 15 scenarios from the [FM operational scenario bank](20-scenario-bank/fm-operational-scenario-bank.md), then refine STAR answers.

## Answer Structure

Use this pattern:

1. Situation: set the FM context.
2. Task: explain the risk or duty.
3. Action: say what you did or would do.
4. Result: show outcome, evidence and learning.

## Longleat-Specific Themes

- Visitor safety under peak footfall.
- Animal welfare infrastructure.
- Fire and life-safety systems.
- Water hygiene governance.
- Suspected asbestos during contractor work.
- Power outage and business continuity.
- Maintenance backlog and reduced budget.
- Board-level risk communication.
