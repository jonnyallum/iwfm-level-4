> Questions 71-80 of the [Longleat interview question bank](../longleat-interview-question-bank.md). Answer aloud, then refine into STAR. Mark with the [spoken-answer rubric](../../17-marking-guides/spoken-answer-rubric.md).

71. What should an asset register contain?
   Strong answer should cover: location, asset type, condition, criticality, compliance tasks and lifecycle.

72. How do you prioritise lifecycle replacement?
   Strong answer should cover: criticality, condition, failure history, cost, lead time and risk.

73. How would you present backlog risk to the board?
   Strong answer should cover: red/amber/green, legal items, options, cost, consequence and decision.

74. What is false economy in FM?
   Strong answer should cover: cutting planned maintenance leading to breakdowns, safety risk and higher cost.

75. How do you decide between repair and replacement?
   Strong answer should cover: age, condition, reliability, parts, risk, lifecycle cost and business impact.

76. How would you reduce maintenance spend responsibly?
   Strong answer should cover: risk-based review, scope challenge, contract performance and protect statutory tasks.

77. What would you do with an incomplete asset register?
   Strong answer should cover: critical assets first, surveys, contractor records and data standards.

78. How do you keep service reports from becoming unread PDFs?
   Strong answer should cover: review, extract defects, assign actions and track closure.

79. What dashboard would you want for asset compliance?
   Strong answer should cover: overdue tasks, critical defects, evidence status, lifecycle risks and contractor performance.

80. How do you handle budget refusal for a high-risk defect?
   Strong answer should cover: document risk, temporary controls, escalate, options and risk acceptance.
