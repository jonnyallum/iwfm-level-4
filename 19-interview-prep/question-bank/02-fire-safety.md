> Questions 11-20 of the [Longleat interview question bank](../longleat-interview-question-bank.md). Answer aloud, then refine into STAR. Mark with the [spoken-answer rubric](../../17-marking-guides/spoken-answer-rubric.md).

11. What is the FM role in fire safety?
   Strong answer should cover: systems, records, inspections, escape routes, actions and competent contractors.

12. What would you do if a fire alarm panel fault appeared during opening hours?
   Strong answer should cover: contractor call, impairment assessment, temporary measures, Responsible Person and records.

13. How would you manage overdue fire risk assessment actions?
   Strong answer should cover: risk rating, ownership, escalation, funding and closure evidence.

14. What would you do if staff kept wedging a fire door open?
   Strong answer should cover: immediate correction, root cause, approved alternatives and monitoring.

15. How do you know whether fire safety records are giving real assurance?
   Strong answer should cover: sample checks, defect close-out and matching records to site condition.

16. How would you manage fire safety during a large event?
   Strong answer should cover: crowd routes, alarms, emergency access, temporary structures and briefing.

17. What is the difference between a fire alarm test and fire alarm servicing?
   Strong answer should cover: user test, competent service, evidence and defect action.

18. How would you respond to a blocked final exit?
   Strong answer should cover: immediate clearance, local accountability, record, recurrence prevention.

19. What compensatory measures might be needed during a fire system impairment?
   Strong answer should cover: fire watch, communication, temporary detection, restrictions and time limit.

20. How would you explain fire door defects to a board member?
   Strong answer should cover: compartmentation, smoke control, escape protection, risk and remedial plan.
