> Questions 51-60 of the [Longleat interview question bank](../longleat-interview-question-bank.md). Answer aloud, then refine into STAR. Mark with the [spoken-answer rubric](../../17-marking-guides/spoken-answer-rubric.md).

51. How do you manage PUWER in an FM team?
   Strong answer should cover: equipment register, suitability, inspections, maintenance and training.

52. What is the difference between lift servicing and LOLER thorough examination?
   Strong answer should cover: maintenance versus independent statutory examination.

53. How do you control work at height?
   Strong answer should cover: avoid, prevent falls, minimise consequences, competence and rescue.

54. When is ladder use acceptable?
   Strong answer should cover: short duration, low risk, suitable ground, inspection and alternatives considered.

55. How would you manage scaffold on a live visitor route?
   Strong answer should cover: segregation, handover, inspection, falling objects and public communication.

56. What would you do if lifting accessories had no ID tags?
   Strong answer should cover: remove from use, inspect, register and competent advice.

57. How do you manage MEWP work near the public?
   Strong answer should cover: exclusion zone, operator competence, rescue plan and communication.

58. What would you do if a contractor changed access method on the day?
   Strong answer should cover: stop, review RAMS, competence, permit and revised controls.

59. How do you prove work equipment users are competent?
   Strong answer should cover: training records, authorisation, supervision and refresher review.

60. How would you respond to a failed LOLER report?
   Strong answer should cover: take out of use if needed, competent advice, remedials and evidence.
