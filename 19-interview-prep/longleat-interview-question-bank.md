## Plain English Summary

This bank contains 100 production interview questions for Longleat-style Facilities Management preparation. The questions focus on operational judgement, visitor safety, animal welfare interfaces, statutory compliance, contractor control, leadership and board-level communication.

For phone-first study the 100 questions are split into ten themed sets of ten. Work one set per session. Use the prompts to practise concise, evidence-led answers in real FM language: risk, control, evidence, ownership, escalation and review.

## How to Use This Bank

- Answer aloud first, then refine into a STAR structure.
- Keep examples practical and site-based.
- Mention evidence, owners and follow-up actions.
- Avoid vague phrases such as "I would look into it" without saying how.
- For Longleat examples, consider visitors, animals, heritage assets, estate roads, contractors and peak-season pressures.
- Mark your spoken answers with the [spoken-answer rubric](../17-marking-guides/spoken-answer-rubric.md).

## Marking Guide

Strong interview answers:

- use clear FM language;
- start with risk and people affected;
- identify immediate controls;
- show evidence and ownership;
- include visitor and animal context where relevant;
- explain escalation without blame;
- close with review and prevention.

Weak answers:

- stay vague;
- ignore evidence;
- overpromise technical knowledge;
- avoid difficult decisions;
- treat public safety as a side issue;
- fail to mention statutory duties.

For a fuller marking standard, use the [marking guide](../17-marking-guides/marking-guide.md) and the [spoken-answer rubric](../17-marking-guides/spoken-answer-rubric.md).
